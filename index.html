📚 The free knowledge: why coastal STRs are a tax strategy (OBBBA edition)

1. 100% bonus depreciation is back — permanently. Cost-seg studies typically reclassify 20–30% of a property into 5/7/15-year assets, all deductible in year one.

2. The 7-day rule. Average guest stays of 7 days or less + material participation = the IRS treats your rental as a business. Losses can offset W-2 and active income — this is why high earners buy beach condos.

3. QBI made permanent. The 20% qualified business income deduction may apply to an STR run as a business.

4. 1031 exchanges untouched. Roll gains into the next property, defer the tax, climb the ladder.

5. Opportunity Zones renewed — including tracts relevant to the Gulf Coast and Alabama.

6. Florida has no state income tax. Your rental income and gains skip state tax entirely.
